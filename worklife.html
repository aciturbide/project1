Full Time Student vs. Full Time Employee?

Many college students balance school and work in order to get a head-start on their future careers and also just essentially have money to provide for themselves. However, many choose to have part-time jobs in order to make it easier on them. However, I am part of the little percentage of students who are actually both full time employees + students. Here's a little glimpse into my crazy life and daily schedules...

My current job and position

Jewel Osco (Cashier + Service Desk + Reciever)

I started working for Jewel Osco back in the summer of 2019 (I will be making 2 years soon!) I started off as a bagger/cashier, you know your typical part-time, easy peasy position.

A couple of months later during the fall of 2020, I was promoted to service desk or as I like to call it a "mini-manager", basically carrying out administrative responsibilies and working directly under our front end manager.

Most recently, I was offered a positon in a completely new department! I am now training and will be becoming the reciever for my Jewel location, meaning I will be responsible for "recieving" our grocery loads and making sure that vendors are delivering and completing the store's orders promptly and accurately.
